  • Simplifying GST Return Filing for Businesses in India

    Goods and Services Tax (GST) has revolutionized the indirect tax system in India, requiring businesses to comply with various regulations, including regular GST return filing. At CredFill, we understand the complexities involved in GST return filing and offer comprehensive services to ensure that your business remains compliant with GST laws.

  • Understanding GST Return Filing

    Under the GST regime, registered taxpayers are required to file various types of GST returns, including monthly, quarterly, and annual returns, depending on their turnover and registration type. These returns contain details of outward supplies, inward supplies, and taxes paid.

  • Types of GST Returns

    The major types of GST returns that businesses need to file include:

    1. GSTR-1: Details of outward supplies of taxable goods and/or services.
    2. GSTR-3B: Summary return of inward and outward supplies, and taxes paid.
    3. GSTR-4: Quarterly return for composition dealers.
    4. GSTR-9: Annual return for regular taxpayers.

  • Who Should File GST Returns?

    All registered taxpayers, including regular taxpayers, composition dealers, and e-commerce operators, are required to file GST returns. The frequency and type of return depend on the taxpayer's turnover and registration type.

  • Benefits of GST Return Filing

    Filing GST returns offers several benefits, including:

    1. Compliance with GST laws and regulations.
    2. Avoidance of penalties and late fees.
    3. Maintenance of a clean tax record.
    4. Access to input tax credit.
    5. Officially verified report of Sales & Purchases.
    6. Helpful in taking loans from Banks or NBFCs.

  • Documents Required for GST Return Filing

    To file GST returns, businesses need to have the following documents:

    1. Sales and purchase invoices.
    2. Details of outward supplies.
    3. Details of inward supplies.
    4. Details of taxes paid.
    5. Bank statements.
    6. Input tax credit details.
